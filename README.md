
Snake — Modern Arcade Edition

Snake is a fast-paced, minimalist arcade game reimagined for today. You control a sleek digital serpent navigating a dynamic grid, collecting food and growing longer with every move.

The objective is simple:
Eat, grow, survive.

As your snake expands, the game becomes increasingly intense. Every turn matters, every second counts, and one wrong move can end your run. It’s a clean, skill-based experience that rewards focus, quick reactions, and smart positioning.

Key Features:

Smooth, responsive controls (Arrow keys or WASD)
Real-time score tracking
Instant restart for continuous play
Classic gameplay with a modern visual feel

Challenge Yourself
How long can you last before the grid becomes your trap?

Snake delivers that timeless “one more round” energy, blending nostalgia with a polished, up-to-date experience. 🚀
